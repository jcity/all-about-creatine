If you’re staring at two tubs of creatine — one standard monohydrate at $15 and one “micronized” version at $25 — the difference is simple chemistry that may or may not matter to you.

## What Micronized Actually Means

Micronized creatine is **creatine monohydrate** with smaller particle size (typically 200 mesh or finer). This doesn’t change the molecule; it changes how the powder behaves in liquid.

- **Solubility:** Smaller particles dissolve more readily in water, resulting in less grittiness and fewer clumps at the bottom of your shaker.
- **Stomach comfort:** A 2021 Cureus randomized controlled trial in active men found that a micronized formulation showed enhanced pharmacokinetics and fewer reports of mild GI discomfort versus conventional monohydrate (https://www.cureus.com/articles/422973).
- **Bioavailability:** Multiple clinical reviews confirm that when total daily dose is the same, muscle creatine uptake is **identical**. Particle size does not change how much creatine ends up in muscle tissue.

## Regular Monohydrate

Standard creatine monohydrate (non-micronized) has the strongest evidence base: hundreds of peer-reviewed trials, decades of use, and the lowest cost per gram. The downsides are simple:

- Can settle at the bottom of liquids if the powder is coarse
- May cause mild bloating in ~5–10% of users at higher loading doses
- Slightly less convenient to mix

## Verdict: Is Micronized Worth It?

**Yes, if:**
- You’re sensitive to texture or stomach upset
- You’re tired of gritty shakes
- You mix creatine in cold water or low-volume drinks

**No, if:**
- Price per gram is your top priority
- You already take monohydrate with no issues
- You use a shaker and don’t mind a few extra seconds of mixing

## Bottom Line

Micronized creatine is the same molecule as regular monohydrate, just milled finer. It mixes better and may sit easier in the stomach, but performance and muscle gains are the same. If you can stomach the grit, save money on regular monohydrate. If you value convenience, micronized is a reasonable luxury.
